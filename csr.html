CSR

At Project Interim, we take our commitment to corporate social responsibility seriously. We are proud of the difference we make and giving back is of paramount importance to us. Members of our team partake in charitable projects throughout the year, positively impacting lives both nationally and internationally. From supporting orphans in Indonesia to helping victims of war across Europe and the Middle East, our commitment to corporate social responsibility lies at the core of our values and principles.

We aim to create a balance between the development of opportunities with our clients, working with and supporting our charitable partners and respecting the environment in which we operate.

Community
Contributing to meaningful and impactful charitable partnerships and activities
Environmental
Supporting sustainable, environmental best practice, minimising carbon footprints
Workplace
Promoting a working environment of positivity, safety, opportunity, health and well-being
Marketplace
Working responsibly with partners to ensure a positive contribution and ethical supply chain
Diversity
Committing to recruiting and selecting a diverse range of applicants
