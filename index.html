Project Interim

We supply proven experts to deliver outstanding results

Project interim are senior level recruiters that specialise in the provision of high calibre Interim, Project, Programme and Change Management resource across several market functions. We support our clients to successfully manage and deliver business change and increase shareholder value.

Combining our knowledge and expertise gained over nearly two decades, and with our insistence on using only the highest quality interim and change professionals, Project Interim is well positioned to support organisations through various challenging and unforeseen circumstances.

As the nature and timing of change becomes ever more complex, the need to identify and install the appropriate skills has never been more critical. We support our clients by recruiting for their interim and change agendas across the UK and in Europe with an open, trustworthy, professional and entirely face-to-face approach.
